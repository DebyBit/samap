Banker's blanket bond Insurance 

            

Banker’s Blanket Bond is a specialist insurance policy which covers  the external and internal risk exposure of banks and other financial  institutions. It protects the assets and liabilities of the banks  against losses from criminal acts performed by their own employees.

This type of insurance provides coverage for the following:
 ●Employee Infidelity
 ●Office, contents and premises protection
 ●Forgery and Falsification of Documents
 ●Stealing & Larceny
 ● Computer and funds transfer fraud
 ●Loss/Damage to property while in transit
 ●Counterfeited currency

There are great risks associated with the operations of banks and  other financial institutions. Therefore, a correct insurance and  comprehensive insurance protection will be able to help these businesses  operate effectively knowing that they have an additional level of  security and protection.

            

            

Jewellers block insurance

           

Because jewelries have high value, simple property insurance cannot provide coverage and protection for it. 

Jeweller’s block is a comprehensive all risk insurance policy for  businesses dealing with precious metals and stones. High value art and  antiques can also be covered in this type of policy. Retailers,  merchandisers and manufacturers of these products are exposed to risk of  theft, burglary, loss and damage to property. 

Jeweler’s Block can specifically cover the following risk exposures:
 –Displays in Showroom
 –Personal Carrying
 –Products in Transit
 –Entrustment to a Third Party

A single loss or damage to your high value properties can have a  great impact on your business. Jeweler’s Block policy can provide you  peace of mind knowing that you have the right protection from any future  disaster.

            

            

Cyber risk insurance

            

Protect your business from financial losses and legal suits arising from cyber crimes and hacking!

Today’s businesses strongly rely on IT infrastructure. From storing  data to performing transactions, everything can be done with the use of  the internet. That is why cyber crimes impose a great risk for all  businesses.

If you are a business which holds sensitive customer details such as  names and addresses or banking information, you might find yourself in  trouble when a hacker gets a hold of this information. You may lose your  customers, but worse, you might find yourself in legal trouble.

 Businesses that rely on their websites to perform their business  transactions can have financial losses due to business interruption when  their websites are shut down by hackers.

Don’t let your business be victimized by these cyber criminals. Insure your business against these cyber risks!
